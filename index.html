Update(01/30/2019):

Just adding the results I obtained from several experiements I have been running. Here the first link points to the results we obtained last week. The main problem there is the checkerboard artifact on most of the output images. I performed one additional experiment with identity loss second link. The results look more or less the same. Link 3 and link 4 shows the results with resize convolution. This gets rid of the checkerboard effect but still have some strange artifacts in the result. Finally, the last two links(link 4, link 5) shows the results without the cycle consistancy with/without identity loss. It appears that CycleGan without identity loss and resize convolution obtained the best results.
